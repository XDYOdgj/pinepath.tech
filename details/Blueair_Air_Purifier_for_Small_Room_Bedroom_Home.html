Blueair Air Purifier - Clean Air in Small Spaces

Experience the difference with the Blueair Air Purifier, designed specifically for compact spaces like your bedroom or living room. Using HEPASilent technology, it efficiently purifies the air by capturing 99.97% of airborne particles as small as 0.1 microns, making it perfect for families, pet owners, and allergy sufferers. This purifier not only improves indoor air quality but also operates quietly, with a low setting of just 19 dB and a maximum of 48 dB, ensuring a peaceful and restful environment.

Smart Features for a Healthier Home

The Blueair Air Purifier includes a dual filtration system that removes dust, pet dander, mold, viruses, and even smoke and cooking odors. With the Blueair app, you can track air quality in real-time, customize cleaning schedules, and get Clean Air ETA updates, giving you full control over your indoor environment. The device also features Night mode with dimmed lights, ideal for undisturbed sleep. Its user-friendly design comes with a simple two-button interface and a child lock for added safety.
